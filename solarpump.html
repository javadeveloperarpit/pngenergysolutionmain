Solar Pump


    Solar Pump


    Solar pumps use energy from solar panels to lift and move water without relying on electricity or diesel. They are eco-friendly, cost-effective, and ideal for agriculture, drinking water, and remote areas.

    How It Works (Simple):
    • Solar panels generate DC power.
    • A controller/inverter powers the pump and manages flow.
    • Pump lifts water from well, bore, or pond.
    • Water stored in tanks or used directly for irrigation/livestock
      •
    Key Features:
    • Runs entirely on free solar energy.
    • Low maintenance and durable system.
    • Reliable water supply in off-grid areas.
    • Environment-friendly with zero emissions.
      •
    Typical Applications:
    • Agriculture irrigation.
    • Drinking water in villages.
    • Livestock watering.
    • Community and industrial use
