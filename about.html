About Our Company

Powering a Sustainable Future with Solar Energy

PNG Energy Solution Pvt. Ltd., Lucknow based renewable energy company, is dedicated to providing innovative and sustainable solar energy solutions. We specialize in designing, supplying, and installing high-quality solar systems for homes, businesses, and industries. With a focus on reliability, efficiency, and affordability, we aim to empower our customers to generate their own electricity, reduce energy costs, and contribute towards a cleaner and greener future. Our team of experts brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the table, making us the go-to choice for all your solar and renewable energy needs. With a focus on staying ahead of the curve, our team members are constantly striving to expand their expertise and stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in the industry.
